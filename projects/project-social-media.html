Social Media Marketing Campaign

How a data-driven content strategy, rigorous A/B testing, and tight KPI tracking delivered +42% engagement and +18% conversions for a client.

Overview

A consumer lifestyle brand wanted to grow their social media presence and drive more direct conversions through Instagram and TikTok. They had tried organic posting before but lacked a structured strategy and were unsure what content actually resonated with their audience.

My role was to design the campaign strategy, develop the content calendar, manage creative briefs, run the A/B tests, and report weekly performance to the client.

Strategy & Execution

The campaign ran over 8 weeks across Instagram and TikTok. I structured it in three phases:

  • Phase 1 — Audit: Analysed 6 months of historical post data to identify top-performing content formats and posting times
  • Phase 2 — Content Production: Developed a 30-day content calendar with 5 distinct content pillars; briefed and reviewed all creative assets
  • Phase 3 — Optimisation: Ran A/B tests on caption style, CTA placement, and post format; reallocated budget to top performers weekly

Results

By week 6, campaign posts were consistently outperforming the baseline. The biggest wins came from short-form video content (TikTok) and carousel posts on Instagram, both of which benefited from stronger hooks and clearer CTAs.

  • +42% average engagement rate across all campaign posts vs. pre-campaign baseline
  • +18% conversion rate on link-in-bio traffic during the campaign window
  • 2.3× increase in profile reach with no additional paid budget
  • Identified the top 3 content formats to anchor the client's ongoing social strategy

Learnings

The biggest insight: consistency of format matters more than frequency. The brand had been posting sporadically across 6 content types. Narrowing to 3 well-executed pillars — education, entertainment, and social proof — drove most of the improvement.
